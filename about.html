My name is Magnolia Sacks, I am a Digital Designer and Developer working remotely for an advertising agency and enjoying the life in the countryside in Port Fairy, Australia.

I’ve spent the past 12+ years working across different areas of digital design; front-end development, email design, marketing site pages, app UI/UX, to my current role designing products for mobile platforms. Today my days are spent researching, designing, prototyping, coding. Out of the office you’ll find me reading Scandinavian crime fiction, playing bass guitar, and petting my three dogs.
